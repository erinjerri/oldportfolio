STAC-SV

Rebranding the Filipino American tech entrepreneur network & Philippine advisory group on innovation

I serve as the Marketing and Communications Director for Science & Technology Advisory Council (STAC) Silicon Valley. Below are a few of the rebranding initiatives I implemented to give a young, fresh makeover to the organization.

The original page flyer is the left, my redesign is to the right. This is for the annual Latitude | Longitude, often referred to as the L2 Conference. Because the conference is intends to be hosted at various locations throughout the world (changing latitude and longitude) year to year, I wanted the rebrand tor relect that from the coordinate references and UI color to reflect branding that would likely be used a submarine navigration-like dashboards.

I wanted to create a clear brand that said our name as the most could not conceive of our acronym. I also wanted to have a motif that represented Silicon Valley (the Silicon chip) in a simplistic design.
