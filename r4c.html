Ro Khanna for Congress

Designing fundraising & campaign experiences

I created and designed fundraising experiences as the sole finance staff for Ro Khanna's Congressional campaign. I designed assets for fundraising as well as other departments for the campaign (work ranged from flyers for various events, banners at campaign rallies to social media content).

The original fundraising page is on the left, my redesign is to the right. Ro Khanna is pictured with me and my family, voters in the California Congressional District-17, the heart of Silicon Valley.

To enhance the experience of click-thru rates and filling out forms (avoiding any more refunds), I mocked up an improved UI for our campaign's grassroots fundraising pages.

I designed our fundraiser invitations and contribution forms.I customized these forms for every host of a fundraiser.

As an early hire, I had the distinct privilege of design our campaign's first round of business cards and took my inspiration from the design of the Obama For America's business cards. This was my business card during the campaign.
